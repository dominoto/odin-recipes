Venison stew with gnocchi

Description

This is a hearty and comforting stew thatâ€™s perfect for a cold winter night. The venison is tender and flavorful, and the gnocchi adds a delightful texture. The stew is infused with fresh rosemary, giving it a wonderful aroma and taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb venison stew meat, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 8 oz mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 sweet onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups beef stock
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 16 oz gnocchi

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, toss the venison with the flour until evenly coated.
  2. Add the venison, mushrooms, onion, garlic, tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, rosemary, bay leaf, and black pepper to your slow cooker along with the beef stock.
  3. Set the slow cooker on high and let cook for 4 hours or set on low heat for 6 hours.
  4. Fifteen minutes prior to serving, add the gnocchi to the slow cooker and stir to combine.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
