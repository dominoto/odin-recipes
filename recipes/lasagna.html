Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family and freezes well, too.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (15-20% fat content)
  • 1 medium onion, finely diced
  • 2 large garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/4 cup dry red wine or beef broth
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Start by making the meat sauce. Cook the ground beef with the onion and garlic until browned.
  2. Add the tomato products (crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste) and sugar to the meat mixture. Season with parsley, basil, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  3. Let the sauce simmer while you boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese with an egg.
  5. To assemble the lasagna, start by spreading a layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Add a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top of the sauce. Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture on top of the noodles. Add a layer of mozzarella cheese on top of the ricotta mixture.
  6. Repeat the layers (meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese) until all ingredients are used up.
  7. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top of the final layer.
  8. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in a preheated oven at 375Â°F for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  9. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving.
